Books by
Dr. Anwaar Ahmed Bugvi

Dr Anwaar Ahmed Bugvi is a writer, scholar and researcher who has investigated the history of Sargodha, Bhera and the Bugvi Family. His extensive work is the five volume series of Tazkaar Bugvia. His travelogues to Central Asia, Middle East and the Subcontinent are also well written. The complete literature from his pen is available on the website.
