Al-Iftikhar Bugvia Foundation

To practice the path of service through labor and commitment for mankind an organization Al Iftikhar Bugvia Foundation was established in 2008 AD by Dr. Anwaar Ahmed Bugvi. The organization is focused on serving the rural ancestral areas of the Bugvi Family in Buggah Sharif and surrounding villages of Khanna, Dhook of District Jhelum. The foundation aims to provide solutions for community problems in the villages related to water, electricity, education, sanitation, cleanliness, health, shelter, clothing, marriage, ration, culture and sports.

Aims and Objectives

  • Propagation of Education and Character Building
  • Health Promotion and Disease Prevention
  • Encouragement for Students in Studies
  • Material Help for Marriage of Girls in Low Income Families
  • Help for Widows. Orphans, Elderly and Calamity Stricken People
  • Protection of Culture of Bhera, Buggah, Khanna and Dhook
  • Encouragement of Local Talent and Skills
  • Promote the Urdu Language and Patronize Literary Activities
  • Support Tolerance, Friendship and Understanding in the Area.
  • Dissipate the teachings and service of Bugvi Ulema / Spiritual Leaders

Strategies

  • Free Medical Camps Related to General, Eyes and Gynecology
  • Cash Awards, Books, Certificates to High Achievers
  • Eid and Ramzan Packages
  • Assistance to Complicated Referred Patients
  • Provision of Clothes for Seasons and Ceremonies.
  • Provision of Dowry and Resources to Women.
  • Free Distribution of Books, Magazines and Newspapers
  • Arranging events for social welfare, religious harmony and social reform
  • Assistance in Higher Education, Career Planning and Job Placement
  • Support of Healthy, Physical and Intellectual Activities
The Al Iftikhar Bugvia Foundation consists of the following office bearers:
Chairman – Vice Chairman – General Secretary – Secretary – Joint Secretary – Finance Secretary – Legal Advisor

The Roles of the Office Bearers have been specified and are established in writing. The membership is open for Men and Women desiring to contribute. The individuals who share the common vision and are aligned with the aims of the Foundation can join on payment of nominal membership fee. The means of income for the Foundation is dependent on contributions by members and prosperous individuals.
