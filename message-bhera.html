Message by the Patrons

With Dua for Allah’s blessings for health and happiness for you and strong belief that he will reward you in this life and the hereafter, the Dar-ul-Uloom Azizia is continuing to function and serve because of your help, contribution and donation. In these times the institute is continuing to serve within its means and budget for the service of the spread and social work in Islam.

This institution has been formed solely from the help and support of kind donors and is continuing work because of your kind attention. The institution has been working for many decades and today the continued zeal and fervour to serve is still present. In these times the services and functions of the institution requires even more energy and strength.

We would strongly appeal to you to continue your support and provide monetary, financial, social and other means of support on continued basis. Your help will support humans in the field of education, health, skill development, Islamic publications and congregations. It is requested to allocate and donate your Zakat, Sadqaa, Khairaat and donation to Majlis Hizb-ul-Ansar, Bhera, District Sargodha.

We will be most thankful to you. Wishing for your continued success and prosperity.
Lastly, the patrons and the whole bugvi.org team would like to thank Col. Zahid Mumtaz for their immense help in providing data to inhabit the website with.
Maulana Abrar Ahmed Bugvi
Dr. Anwaar Ahmed Bugvi
